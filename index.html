About FoodBank

FoodBank is built to reduce avoidable food waste and provide nourishment to those who need it most. Restaurants can list surplus, volunteers collect, and donations get distributed to community points.

Vision

Our vision is a hunger-free local community where excess food is reused, not wasted.

Mission

Build an easy-to-use platform to connect donors, volunteers, and recipients — ensuring safe, efficient, and timely redistribution of food.
